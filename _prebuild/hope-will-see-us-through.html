Typography & Music

Hope Will See Us Through is small side experiment I started in early 2011. I was listening to a song, Its Not A Bad Little War by Bayside, and at some point in the breakdown, I realized that I had listened to it over and over, but I had never actually heard it.

So, I started focusing more on lyrics in some of my favorite albums and create short, quick type experiments.
