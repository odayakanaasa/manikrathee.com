A moment, infinite.

I love being around happy people. I love seeing people smile their biggest smiles as they start their lives together and to know that it is one of the best moments that they will ever experience. I love being the person that allows those memories to live on forever. My name is Manik Rathee and I photograph love.

Photography

Photography isn’t a job to me – it is my passion. I spend eight, nine, sometimes over ten hours, on my feet photographing weddings and engagement parties, but during all of that time, it never becomes “work”.

Manik Rathee Photography is not a big company or a slew of photographers. It’s just me. You send a message through the contact form and it comes right to my phone. We schedule our first meeting and I’m the person there greeting you with a smile. I operate with full transparency – if you want to book me, you don’t just book the name or the style of photos I shoot – you book someone that is going to become a friend over the next few weeks. I will learn what makes you, well, you - and that, as simple as it sounds, will make your photos perfect.
