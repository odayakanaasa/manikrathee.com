Minimal & Beautiful

CATALYST was a minimal iOS Theme designed for the very first iPhone with Retina Display.

The Spec

CATALYST was designed for the iPhone 4 but it was also compatible with previous generation iPhones, iPods as well as iPads. The theme covered native applications and a large number of third-party apps. It also included one of the first widgets on an iOS theme.

Cydia Packages

Cydia and some of its most popular packages are skinned under CATALYST. This number will be growing as more options become available to the public.

The Apps you know & love

The Apple iPhone is known for its fantastic form factor, amazing operating systems and, most of all, the incredible library of apps that we have come to love. CATALYST covers hundreds of these applications and has more added constantly. From productivity apps like OmniFocus, to music streaming apps like Rdio and Pandora, to social apps like Facebook, Twitter and Google+ - everything you know and love can fall within the gorgeous & minimalistic umbrella of CATALYST.
